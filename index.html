Biography

I am a Master student in the Department of Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering at University of Waterloo. Currently, I am working towards my Master’s thesis on Real Gas Effects on Compressible flows in Supercritical regime in Multiphysics Interation lab under the supervision of Professor Jean-Pierre Hickey.

I opened this website to keep track of my graduate research life, academic courses, and last but not the least, ideas that I want to share with others. I was born and raised in Andhra Pradesh, India. Telugu (తెలుగు) is my first language.

Projects

*
All Computational Fluid Dynamics Supercritical Fluids Algorithmic trading Finance Data Science

Adaptive Mesh refinement based Tabulated equation of state

A tabulation based Equation of state generated adaptively based on Quadtree datastructure and user defined Error Tolerance.

Financial Portfolio Management and Statistical Analysis of S&P 500 traded securities

Computation of daily returns and descriptive statistics of 3 US car stocks(F, GM, TSLA) over a 5 year period using Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT) to formulate efficient portfolios from individual assets and compute portfolio frontier. I implemented Random Walk (RW) and ARIMA time series model to simulate time history of stock price for forecasting

Improving 311 service through GeoSpatial Analysis and Time series Analysis

This is our team’s submission to Citadel Data Open competition, through our anlysis we tried to answer what suggestions can we make to improve the resolution efficiency of 311 service (a non emergency call service for the New York city) requests.

Multi-class Classification Algorithms for Hand Written Digits

A python code for 3 layer perceptron algorithm to classify hand written digits from MNIST database.

Pairs Trading with Machine Learning

One popular trading algorithm is Pairs Trading, in this strategy finding valid, eligible pairs that show unconditional mean-reversion behaviour play a main role. This project aims to implement an approach to find such eligible pairs by using Machine learning to navigate very high-dimensional stock search space to find eligible trading pairs

Prediction of Santander Bank's customer spending behaviour

Prediction of customer purchase behaviour and implementation of recommendation system using santander bank’s dataset on monthly data of customer transactions for 1.5 years

CFD modelling of heat transfer in Geothermal heat exchanger

Experimental and numerical modeling analyses of heat transfer processes in the ground source heat exchangers.

Real fluid effects on Shock Tube flow at supercritical conditions

A study on shock tube simulations under supercritical thermodynamic conditions to investigate real fluid effects on the wave propagation.
